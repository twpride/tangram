How to play

  • To start a puzzle, click on a level in the selector (at the bottom right)
  • Move the puzzle pieces until they match the shape in the silhouette (top left)
    • All seven pieces must be used and they cannot overlap
  • Can you solve the puzzle under five minutes? one?
    • Each box in the level selector is colored based on the elapsed time (see color bar in legend)
    • Challenge yourself and others to get as many green boxes as possible!

Moving the puzzle pieces

  • Translation
    • Mouse: Left click and drag
    • Touch: Tap and drag
  • Rotation
    • Mouse: Right click and drag
    • Two-finger touch: With 1 finger touching the piece, drag with second finger
    • One-finger touch: Double tap piece. Drag on second tap
  • Lifting a piece up
    • Mouse: double click
    • With touch: long press
    • Lifted pieces can move over (overlap) other pieces
  • Flipping the parallelogram
    • Click or tap the button below the play/pause button.
